01 — Essence

Intelligence that connects everything.

The brand stands for connective intelligence — the platform that links Indonesia's enterprises, institutions, and innovators through AI, automation, and secure infrastructure. Every visual choice flows from this idea.

Pillar 01

Bold, never loud.

Confident weights and tight spacing — the brand asserts authority without shouting. Boldness is structural, not decorative.

Pillar 02

Modern, never trendy.

Built on enduring sans-serif systems and a disciplined grid. The look should still feel right in five years.

Pillar 03

Professional, never sterile.

Enterprise-grade clarity with warmth in the spacing, the proof points, the human voice.

Pillar 04

Indonesian, globally legible.

Rooted in Jakarta, designed to read alongside Stripe, Linear, and Vercel — not behind them.

The mark.

A rounded square symbol locks up with a tight wordmark. It can stand alone as the symbol, or run alongside the wordmark for full brand presence.

01 · Letterform

Geist 900 Black at display sizes, set tight at -0.05em tracking. The "L" and "ab" carry the accent in primary blue.

02 · Closing punctuation

The full stop is functional, not decorative — it signals authority and finality. Use it on hero treatments only, never inline.

03 · Clearspace

Maintain a clearspace equal to the height of the lowercase "o" on every side. Never let other elements crowd the mark.

03 — Type

Geist, used decisively.

One typeface, the full weight range. Geist is a precision-engineered sans-serif built for the digital age — tight geometric letterforms, exceptional clarity at every size, and a confident voice that reads as intentional. Bold for headlines, regular for body, mono for technical accents.

04 — Color

A disciplined palette.

Black for authority, white for clarity, one decisive blue for focus. Neutrals carry the rest. The palette is intentionally tight — restraint creates recognition.

Light surface · Primary use

Confidence reads in white space.

The default canvas. Generous margins, ink for text, blue for emphasis. Avoid layering decoration — let typography do the work.

Dark surface · Strategic use

Authority reads in contrast.

Reserve dark sections for tonal punctuation — values, manifesto moments, footer. Don't overuse; the page should breathe in white.

05 — Voice

Direct, declarative, human.

We speak the way our headlines look — bold, plain, unhedged. No corporate-speak, no hype, no jargon dressed up as expertise. Active voice. Present tense. Specific over abstract.

Do · Direct & specific
We connect Indonesia's enterprises to the AI ecosystem they need.

Active. Concrete. Names what we do, for whom.

Don't · Corporate & abstract
ConextLab Technology endeavors to facilitate digital transformation through innovative AI-driven paradigms.

Passive. Generic. Could be any company in any industry.

Do · Confident, not hyped
Twenty technology partners. Five divisions. One ecosystem.

Numbers earn the claim. Punctuation does the heavy lifting.

Don't · Adjective stack
A revolutionary, world-class, cutting-edge AI ecosystem like nothing you've ever seen before.

Stacked superlatives say nothing. The reader switches off.

06 — In Use

The system, applied.

A preview of how the brand reads on the homepage and on a business card. Same type. Same color. Same voice. Always recognisable.
